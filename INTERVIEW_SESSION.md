# Interview Session: COMPAS Case Study
## SQE Senior Product Manager - AI & Enterprise Solutions

**Candidate:** Tata (Banat Zata)
**Date:** December 2024
**Position:** Senior Product Manager - AI & Enterprise Solutions

---

## Product Selection

### Q: Target Market untuk Compliance Monitoring?
**A:** Both Financial Services & Mining simultaneously - build generic platform yang bisa serve kedua industry dari awal.

**Follow-up:** "Caranya gimana ya?"

---

## Undebatable Reasons: Why Compliance Monitoring over Market Intelligence

### Q: Core Arguments untuk pilih Compliance?
**A:** ALL FOUR selected:
1. **Regulatory Urgency** - New regulations 2025 = market HARUS beli
2. **Built-in Customers** - 40+ Sinarmas subsidiaries = guaranteed pilot
3. **Technical Feasibility** - Defined rules vs infinite variables
4. **Quantifiable ROI** - Compliance fines are REAL money saved

### Tata's Additional Insight:
> "Dengan adanya compliance dan SOP, sangat memudahkan untuk audit. Orang jadi 'bersinergi dan kompak' karena punya SOP/Governance yang jelas demi comply. Struktur kerja terstandardisasi, hasil pasti standar, meminimalisir kesalahan. Kalaupun ada kesalahan, sudah di-mapping risk-nya dan risk register-nya. Dengan SOP yang benar dan comply = efisien."

---

## Core AI Features

### Q: Core AI Feature untuk PRD?
**A:** Semua penting, tapi:
- No 1 (Real-time Deviation Detection) & No 4 (Smart SOP Recommendation) terasa redundan
- Butuh yang lebih "GONG"

### Q: 'Gong' Factor - What makes it special?
**A:** ALL FOUR selected:
1. **Regulatory Change Auto-Adapt** - AI auto-update SOP ketika regulasi berubah
2. **Cross-Entity Benchmarking** - Compare compliance across 40+ subsidiaries
3. **Natural Language Query** - Tanya AI dalam Bahasa Indonesia
4. **Gamification & Compliance Score** - Leaderboard, rewards

### Tata's Concern on Auto-Adapt:
> "No 1 itu pisau mata dua. Kalau emang update SOP terus, gimana people dan change management-nya?"

---

## Change Management Approach

### Q: Flow untuk Regulatory Auto-Adapt?
**A:** ALL FOUR selected:
1. **AI Suggest → Human Approve** - HARUS ada approval dari Compliance Officer
2. **Staged Rollout** - Test di 1 branch/dept dulu, baru rollout
3. **Training Auto-Trigger** - Auto mandatory training setiap SOP change
4. **Grace Period System** - Transition period 30-60 hari sebelum enforced

---

## User Persona

### Q: Primary User yang pakai day-to-day?
**A:**
> "COO group SMMA (kalau ada) sama manager level keatas karena buat make sure anak-anaknya comply. Lebih oke lagi kalau setiap dokumen yang terkait compliance, governance dan SOP bisa dicek sudah comply sesuai aturan pakai AI. Ada AI assistant yang pakai bahasa manusia."

### Q: Metrics paling penting?
**A:** Semua 4 benar:
1. Compliance Score Improvement

3. Audit Time Reduction
4. Incident Reduction

### Tata's Challenge:

## AI Risk & Mitigation

### Q: AI Failure Modes priority?
**A:** All selected, but:
> "To be honest gue gatau. Bisa kah lu research dan give me insight, bukan tentang apps yang ready di pasaran tapi yang bener-bener C-Level dan perusahaan butuhkan. Baik terkait AI risk-nya maupun potential-nya. Kalau ada risk kira-kira apa solusinya?"

### Q: AI Risk Mitigation Priority (after research)?
**A:** ALL selected + tambahan:
1. **Explainability First** - SHAP/LIME, audit trail lengkap
2. **Human-in-the-Loop** - Always approval layer
3. **Model Versioning & Lineage** - Track setiap perubahan
4. **Confidence Scoring** - Low confidence = escalate to human
5. **Rollout and Rollback capability**
